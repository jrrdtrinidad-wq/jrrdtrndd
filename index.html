About Goldilocks Philippines

Goldilocks and Goldilocks SM City Tuguegarao are part of Goldilocks Bakeshop, Inc. — the leading Filipino bakery and food service company that has become a staple in Filipino celebrations and everyday life.

Our Story & Heritage

Goldilocks began with a simple dream in 1966 when two sisters Milagros Leelin Yee and Clarita Leelin Go and their sister-in-law Doris Wilson Leelin opened a modest bakeshop in Makati.

Since then, the brand has grown into the largest Filipino-owned bakeshop chain in the world.
